Back to Bridges

Drilling and build of 3 strategic bridges in West Nile Sub region: Kochi Bridge on Keri-Midigo-Kerwa Road, Osu on Arua- Biliafe- Otrevu Raod and Odrua on Arua- Vuraa Custom-Paidha Goli Road.

Kochi Bridge spans River Kochi, located in Yumbe District in West Nile sub‑region of Uganda. It’s positioned on the Yumbe–Moyo Road, it serves as a vital link for communities accessing markets, schools, health facilities,and even cross-border connections with South Sudan. 

Challenge

The original bridge constructed in the early 1960s was a narrow wooden-decked structure that became increasingly unsafe. Rotting timber and multiple accidents as vehicles plunged into the river were highly noted.

Solution

The Ugandan government secured approximately Shs 8.2 billion for a comprehensive rebuild. Dynaco Engineering was contracted to carry out the design and construction over a three-year span. The project includes replacing the old structure, performing interim maintenance, and opening/tarmacking nearby roads. The new bridge is engineered to have a 100‑year lifespan. GETLAB was contracted to conduct the geotechnical investigations to aid in proper Foundation Design and stability. Activities entailed Rotary Drilling, test pit excavations, DCP tests, Material Testing and Reporting.

Results

Findings from the geotechnical investigations aided informed decision making of the design and construction teams.
